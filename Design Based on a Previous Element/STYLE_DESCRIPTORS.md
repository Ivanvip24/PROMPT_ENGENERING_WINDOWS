# Style Descriptor Library

## Purpose
Pre-written style descriptions to copy-paste instead of writing custom aesthetic essays each time. Use these proven descriptors that generate consistent results.

**Usage:** Pick ONE primary style per design. Copy descriptor directly into prompt's "Style & Aesthetic" section.

---

## Cartoon & Playful Styles

### Whimsical Cartoon
**Copy-paste descriptor:**
"Playful cartoon illustration with bold black outlines (3-4pt), flat vibrant colors, friendly character design, simple shapes with personality, cheerful approachable mood celebrating [location] through family-friendly joyful lens."

**Visual characteristics:**
- Bold black outlines around all elements
- Flat colors with minimal shading
- Simplified friendly shapes
- Expressive faces/features
- High saturation colors

**Best for:** Family souvenirs, character-focused designs, playful cultural celebrations, children's appeal

**Example color palettes:** Bright primaries, hot pinks, turquoise, sunny yellows, vibrant oranges

---

### Cute Kawaii
**Copy-paste descriptor:**
"Adorable kawaii style with rounded shapes, pastel color palette, big expressive eyes, simplified cute forms, soft gentle aesthetic creating sweet memorable [location] charm with universal appeal."

**Visual characteristics:**
- Everything rounded, no sharp edges
- Pastel soft colors
- Oversized cute features
- Minimal details
- Sweet innocent feel

**Best for:** Gift items, younger audiences, cute animal mascots, soft cultural interpretation

**Example color palettes:** Pastel pink, mint green, baby blue, lavender, peach, cream

---

### Bold Pop Art
**Copy-paste descriptor:**
"Bold pop art illustration with strong graphic shapes, high contrast colors, thick outlines, Ben-Day dots or halftone patterns, energetic contemporary aesthetic bringing modern vibrant energy to [location]."

**Visual characteristics:**
- Strong geometric shapes
- High contrast (often complementary colors)
- Comic book influence
- Halftone/dot patterns
- Dynamic bold energy

**Best for:** Modern urban destinations, contemporary art markets, young adult appeal, bold statements

**Example color palettes:** Hot pink + cyan, yellow + purple, red + blue high contrast combinations

## Realistic & Detailed Styles

### Realistic Illustrative

## Quick Selection Guide

**Use this decision tree:**

**Budget/Family-friendly souvenirs?**
→ Whimsical Cartoon, Cute Kawaii

**Sophisticated/premium products?**
→ Realistic Illustrative, Vintage Naturalist, Art Nouveau

**Classic timeless appeal?**
→ Vintage Travel Poster, Retro 1970s

**Cultural authenticity emphasis?**
→ Decorative Folk Art, Indigenous Textile, Mexican Papel Picado

**Modern/contemporary audience?**
→ Modern Flat Design, Geometric Abstract, Minimalist Line Art

**Spiritual/mystical themes?**
→ Mystical Atmospheric, Stained Glass

**Bold/dramatic statement?**
→ Bold Pop Art, Noir Dramatic, Neon Glow

**Handmade/artisanal feel?**
→ Watercolor Soft, Woodcut Print, Decorative Folk Art

## Mixing Styles (Advanced)

Can combine compatible styles:

**Compatible Combinations:**
- Vintage Travel Poster + Decorative Folk Art = Vintage with folk patterns
- Realistic Illustrative + Mystical Atmospheric = Realistic with magical lighting
- Whimsical Cartoon + Mexican Papel Picado = Cartoon with cut-paper aesthetic
- Modern Flat Design + Geometric Abstract = Flat geometric patterns
- Watercolor Soft + Vintage Naturalist = Soft painted botanical study

**Incompatible Combinations (Avoid):**
- Realistic Illustrative + Whimsical Cartoon = Conflicting detail levels
- Minimalist Line Art + Decorative Folk Art = Opposite decoration philosophies
- Neon Glow + Watercolor Soft = Conflicting color approaches
- Modern Flat + Vintage Travel Poster = Era conflict

## Customization Template

To customize any descriptor:

**Base descriptor** + **[intensity modifier]** + **[cultural specificity]** + **[mood emphasis]**

**Example:**
"Whimsical cartoon illustration with bold outlines and flat colors" +
"with EXTRA vibrant saturation" +
"celebrating Oaxacan cultural traditions" +
"creating joyful festive mood"

= "Whimsical cartoon illustration with bold black outlines, extra vibrant flat colors celebrating Oaxacan cultural traditions with joyful festive mood."

## Success Metrics

You're using style descriptors correctly when:
- ✅ Can select style in under 30 seconds
- ✅ Copy-paste descriptor without rewriting
- ✅ Add 0-2 minor customizations maximum
- ✅ Total style section = 2-3 sentences
- ✅ No technical specs (CMYK, fonts, line weights)
- ✅ Style matches composition framework choice
- ✅ Style appropriate for target audience

---

## Pro Tips

1. **Match style to audience:** Family = Cartoon, Sophisticated = Realistic/Vintage
2. **Consider production:** Laser-cut works better with bold outlines (Cartoon, Folk Art, Woodcut)
3. **Color complexity:** Simple styles = simple palettes, Complex styles = richer palettes
4. **Cultural sensitivity:** Use culturally appropriate styles (Folk Art for indigenous destinations)
5. **Uniqueness:** Vary style choices even for same destination to create unique designs
