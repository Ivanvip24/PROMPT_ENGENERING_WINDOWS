# Tenango de las Flores Sunflower Frame Design

## AGENT 1: ELEMENT ANALYSIS

### Element Type
**Frame/Border Element** - Decorative sunflower frame that will contain a scenic photograph

### Visual Analysis
- **Art Style**: Hand-illustrated, warm and cheerful folk art style
- **Colors**: Golden yellows (C0 M20 Y90 K0), orange (C0 M50 Y90 K0), dark brown centers (C30 M60 Y80 K60), green leaves (C60 M20 Y100 K10)
- **Pattern**: Sunflowers arranged as decorative border around empty center
- **Detail Level**: Moderate - simplified petals, textured centers, organic leaves
- **Line Quality**: Soft, organic, hand-drawn quality with subtle variations
- **Composition**: Frame format with empty center space for photograph

### Frame Characteristics
- **Shape**: Rectangular frame with organic, irregular border
- **Distribution**: Sunflowers denser at corners, scattered along edges
- **Spacing**: Open center (approximately 60% of total area) for photo placement
- **Connection**: Flowers connected by green leaf stems and small decorative leaves
- **Accent Elements**: Small yellow star sparkles scattered throughout

### Element Purpose
This is a **FRAME element** designed to surround and enhance a central photograph, not a standalone character. The design strategy is different from character-based designs.

### Composition Recommendations
- Place scenic photograph of Tenango de las Flores in center (60% of design area)
- Keep sunflower frame intact as border (40% of design area)
- Add location typography integrated with frame or photo
- Maintain 1:1 square format
- Frame should enhance but not compete with central photograph
