## üéØ PATTERN CATEGORIES

### 1. CONTAINER TRANSFORMATIONS
**Place the entire design inside something**

#### Inside Glass/Transparent Objects
- **Snow globe** - Elements float inside sphere
- **Message in bottle** - Design compressed inside glass
- **Diving mask reflection** - Scene reflected in lens
- **Coffee cup** - Design floating on coffee surface
- **Fishbowl/Aquarium** - Underwater scene contained

#### Inside Solid Objects
- **Open book pages** - Pop-up book concept
- **Puzzle pieces** - Design fragmented across pieces
- **Calendar wheel** - Circular Mayan-style arrangement
- **Shell segments** - Opens like market display

**Why these work**: Forces automatic 3D depth and complete restructuring

---

### 2. JOURNEY/PATH PATTERNS
**Create movement through the composition**

#### Linear Journeys
- **Treasure map** - Dotted path connecting destinations
- **Road/highway** - Elements along winding road
- **Timeline** - Chronological progression
- **Film strip** - Sequential frames

#### Circular/Spiral Journeys
- **Nautilus spiral** - Growing progression
- **Circular wreath** - Elements orbit center
- **Carousel** - Rotating arrangement
- **Clock face** - Time-based positioning

**Why these work**: Creates natural flow and dynamic energy

---

### 3. PERSPECTIVE SHIFTS
**Change the viewing angle completely**

#### Angle Changes
- **Front view ‚Üí Side profile**
- **Ground level ‚Üí Aerial/bird's eye**
- **Flat ‚Üí 3/4 perspective**
- **Static ‚Üí In motion**

#### Depth Transformations
- **2D flat ‚Üí 3D layered**
- **Single plane ‚Üí Multiple depth planes**
- **Foreground/background separation**
- **Inside-out view**

**Why these work**: Same elements look completely different from new angle

---

### 4. FRAGMENTATION PATTERNS
**Break design into separate pieces**

#### Scattered Arrangements
- **Polaroid photos** - Multiple angled snapshots
- **Postcards** - Overlapping travel cards
- **Torn paper pieces** - Collage effect
- **Floating islands** - Separated land masses

#### Systematic Divisions
- **Grid/mosaic** - Organized tiles
- **Puzzle pieces** - Interlocking segments
- **Comic panels** - Sequential boxes
- **Window panes** - Framed sections

**Why these work**: Breaks monotony, creates visual interest

---

### 5. SHAPE TRANSFORMATIONS
**Change the overall silhouette**

#### From Rectangle To:
- **Circle/oval** - Radial composition
- **Triangle** - Pyramid arrangement
- **Diamond/rhombus** - Diagonal energy
- **Organic shape** - Natural flowing edges
- **Object shape** - Surfboard, bottle, tree

**Why these work**: Different shape = different design instantly

---

### 6. PHYSICS/LOGIC INVERSIONS
**Break normal rules creatively**

#### Gravity Defiance
- **Upside-down world** - Everything inverted
- **Floating elements** - Weightless arrangement
- **Water flowing up** - Reversed physics
- **Sky as ground** - Flipped reality

#### Scale Play
- **Miniature in giant object** - Tiny world concept
- **Giant element with tiny surroundings**
- **Nested scales** - Design within design

**Why these work**: Memorable, unexpected, forces creativity

## üöÄ QUICK SELECTION GUIDE

### For Level 3-4 (Subtle)
- Perspective shift (front ‚Üí side)

### For Level 5-6 (Moderate)

### For Level 7-8 (Bold)

- Multi-stage transformation

### For Level 9-10 (Radical)

- Surreal combinations
