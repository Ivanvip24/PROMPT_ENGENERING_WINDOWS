# CDMX Design Variations

**Destination**: Ciudad de México (Mexico City)
**Key Icon**: Ángel de la Independencia (Angel of Independence)

   - Golden Ángel de la Independencia as hero

   - Text: "CDMX"
   - Elegant sophisticated aesthetic

   - Decoration Level: 8/10

3. **cdmx_angel_art_deco_night.md** - Horizontal format (10" × 5") [NEW CLIENT ORDER]

   - Decoration Level: 8/10

4. **cdmx_angel_botanical_sunset.md** - Horizontal format (10" × 5") [NEW CLIENT ORDER]

## Design Comparison

**All designs feature**: Ángel de la Independencia as protagonist, golden monument tones

**Completely different approaches**:
- **Folk Art Garden**: Mexican maximalism, vibrant folk art patterns, abundant flowers
- **Art Deco Night**: Geometric precision, urban sophistication, nighttime cityscape

Each design is unique for different client orders.

## Style Variations

CDMX designs can explore:
- **Folk Art**: Vibrant patterns, papel picado, Talavera tiles, maximalist decoration
- **Art Deco**: Geometric precision, stepped forms, urban sophistication, metallic elegance
- **Botanical**: Natural abundance, romantic atmosphere, garden beauty, floral richness
- **Modern**: Clean lines, bold colors, contemporary graphic design
- **Traditional**: Classic Mexican aesthetic, cultural celebration
