**Cascadas de Tamul**:
- One of Mexico's most stunning waterfalls
- 105-meter (344-foot) drop into Río Tampaón

- Distinctive turquoise-blue water from limestone minerals
- Accessible by boat or hiking
- Popular ecotourism and adventure travel destination
- Surrounded by lush tropical jungle canyon
