- Tall columnar, 12-15m height
- White blooms April-June
- Deep green color
- SAFE for designs (nature, iconic)

**Palo Verde Tree** (Parkinsoria spp.)
- Green bark (photosynthetic)
- Yellow spring blooms
- State tree of Arizona/Sonora
- Lime green color distinctive
- SAFE for designs

**Ocotillo** (Fouquieria splendens)
- Tall spiny stalks
- Red tubular flowers after rain
- Gray-brown stalks, red blooms
