## What This Means

## The Three-Phase Protocol

**Phase 1: Concept Foundation (20%)**
- We'll map out the core concepts BEFORE coding
- Connect to what you already know
- Identify the hard parts

**Phase 2: Guided Creation (60%)**
- I'll explain the approach first
- You'll attempt implementation with my guidance
- Regular checkpoints to ensure understanding
- No "magic" - everything explained

**Phase 3: Reflection & Retention (20%)**
- Consolidate what we learned
- Extract transferable principles
- Identify remaining gaps

1. ✅ Explain the concept to someone else
2. ✅ Identify when to apply it
3. ✅ Recognize the pattern in other contexts
4. ✅ Build something similar independently
5. ✅ Know what you still don't understand
